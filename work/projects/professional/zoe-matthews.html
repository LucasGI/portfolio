Project Description

Some authors are now making apps that their fan base can download and be better involved with the author and current news. I made this app for Zoe Matthews, a romance author.

The purpose of this app is to add a new way for Zoe to communicate with her readers. This app has links to all of her content and books. One main feature of this app is Google Cloud Messaging (GCM), where Zoe can send push notifications to everyone who has the app downloaded.
